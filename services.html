The Core Offering

Weekly ThndrStrm
Strategy Sessions

Once a week, you get one focused hour with Kaliko Castille. No preset agenda. No curriculum to follow. You bring what's most pressing that week — a decision you're wrestling with, a message that isn't landing, a campaign you're building, a problem your team keeps running into — and you work through it together.

Between sessions, a shared working document keeps track of everything on your radar. Questions, ideas, topics, half-formed thoughts — drop them in as they come up so nothing gets lost and every session starts sharp.

What ThndrStrm actually does

ThndrStrm helps you think more clearly, decide more confidently, and build the internal capacity to execute without permanent outside dependence.

The goal of every engagement is to ultimately make outside help unnecessary — to leave your team smarter, more strategic, and fully capable of running without us.

What you walk away with every week

  • Clarity on your most pressing strategic challenge
  • A sharper read on the landscape you're operating in
  • The frameworks to move forward confidently without waiting for someone else to hand you the answer
Scope

What sessions
typically cover

Depending on where you are and where you're trying to go, Weekly ThndrStrm Strategy Sessions typically touch on some combination of the following — though you don't need to come in with a problem that fits neatly into any category. You just need to come in ready to think.

Digital Strategy & Communications

How do you show up online in a way that actually builds trust and drives action rather than just adding noise?

Content & Messaging

What story are you telling, who are you telling it to, and is it landing the way you think it is?

Campaign Strategy

Whether it's an electoral campaign, an advocacy push, or a product launch — how do you build the conditions for momentum?

Organizational Strategy

Where is your team's energy actually going, and is it pointed at the right things?

Fundraising Strategy

How do you build a pipeline that doesn't feel like begging, and a case for support that actually converts?

Coalition & Community Building

How do you find your people, earn their trust, and turn them into a force that moves things?

Clarity

A few things worth
naming clearly.

Weekly ThndrStrm Strategy Sessions are not an execution service. ThndrStrm doesn't write your content, run your social media, or manage your campaigns. There are good vendors for all of those things — and part of what we'll do together is help you figure out which ones you actually need and how to get the most out of them.

If you're looking for someone to do the work for you, this isn't the right fit.

If you're looking for a thought partner who will help you understand your strategy deeply enough to own it — and build a team that can run it — let's talk.

Availability

A note on availability.

ThndrStrm takes on a limited number of clients at any given time — not as a scarcity tactic, but because quality requires focus. When you work with ThndrStrm, you're not sharing attention with dozens of other organizations. You're getting a strategic partner who knows your situation, remembers your history, and shows up every week ready to go deep.

Current availability is limited. If you're interested, apply early.
